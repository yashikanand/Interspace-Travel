Long Trip

3-week plan

In a three-week plan we’re offering 15 days 15 nights of stay with six days of traveling to any three planets you desire. The stay time can be less if you choose the planets which are far away from each other as it can take some more time to reach the destination.

4-week plan

In a four-week plan, we’re offering 20 days 20 nights of stay with eight days of traveling to any four planets you desire. The stay time can be less if you choose the planets which are far away from each other as it can take some more time to reach the destination.

5-week plan

The five-week plan is a special one as you can get to travel all the planets with this plan. In this plan, we offer 25 days 25 nights of stay time and ten days of traveling. With this offer, you actually get to stay for 25 days as we’re traveling to all the planets and we will go there in a sequence so no time will be wasted.
